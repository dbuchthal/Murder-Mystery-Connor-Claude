# Round 2 — Contessa Savoca

the art lovers. *Che volgarità.* You want only to be gone from this place.

- The poison was a **green substance.**
- There's a forger in Dolan's collection.

drift wherever it likes, so long as it stays far from you. Conveniently, you know a thing or
two about a couple of these people that would make *fine* distractions.

  call** — a secretly desperate executive is a tidy suspect.
- You've heard the whisper that "**Walken**" is a stage name and the man is **broke** behind
  the glamour. **Needle him about his real identity and his finances.**
