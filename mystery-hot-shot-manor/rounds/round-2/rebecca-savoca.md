# Round 2 — Contessa Savoca

## What just happened
Murder, *naturalmente.* Poison — some squalid green pigment. And a forger lurking among the
art lovers. *Che volgarità.* You want only to be gone from this place, but the storm and the
corpse conspire to keep you.

## What you now know
- The weapon was an artist's pigment.
- There's a forger in Dolan's collection.
- Antoninski is sweating like a guilty man — and his troubles could become *your* troubles
  if the sasquatch business surfaces.

## This round
Stay above it all. Quietly hope Antoninski keeps his composure — if his fraud comes out,
your bribe is at risk. Reveal nothing of your bankruptcy or the arrangement with him. Let
suspicion drift wherever it likes, so long as it stays far from you.

## If pressed
You retired to your room and passed through the parlor where Walken performed — others saw
you. Reveal nothing of the Nobel arrangement or your finances. If Antoninski looks likely to
be voted guilty, you may *subtly* encourage him to "clear himself" (you'd rather his alibi
than his fraud — but tread carefully).
