# Round 2 — Dr. Connor Dolan (Ghost)

## What just happened
Buck has told them the truth: you were *murdered,* with a green poison in your buttermilk. Your
spectral pride is wounded — done in by something the color of pond scum.

## This round (your role)
- Haunt and grumble. You may moan about being "done in by an emerald lie" or that "a green hand
  mixed my last drink" — atmospheric hints only. **Do not name the poison** (even you, the
  ghost, keep that mystery — it's a Round 4 reveal).
- Keep **quietly flagging lies to the host.** When someone's alibi contradicts the true
  timeline (you know it from the bible), groan for the host's benefit — don't expose it to the
  table.
- Do **not** reveal the killer, the kit/glove, or anyone's specific secret.

## Tip for the host
If the forgery thread stalls, have Dolan lament "my poor stolen paintings" to nudge players
toward the art angle.
