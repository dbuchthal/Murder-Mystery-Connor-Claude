# Round 2 — Dr. Connor Dolan (Ghost)

## What just happened
Buck has told them the truth: you were *murdered,* with a green poison in your buttermilk. Your
spectral pride is wounded — done in by something the color of pond scum, by one of a houseful of
people who *all,* it seems, keep poison in their bags.

## This round (your role)
- Haunt and grumble. You may moan about being "done in by an emerald lie" — atmospheric only.
  **Do not name the poison** (even the ghost keeps that mystery; it's an R4 reveal).
- Keep **quietly flagging lies to the host.** When an alibi contradicts the true timeline, groan
  for the host's benefit — never expose it to the table.
- Do **not** reveal the killer or anyone's specific secret. Savor the parade of confessions.

## Tip for the host
If the forgery thread stalls, lament "my poor *painted-over* masterpieces" to nudge players
toward the *painting*-forgery angle (it matters in the end).
