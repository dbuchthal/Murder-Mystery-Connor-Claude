# Round 2 — Dr. Connor Dolan (Ghost)

## What just happened
Buck has told them the truth: you were *murdered,* with Paris Green in your buttermilk. Your
spectral pride is wounded — poisoned with *paint,* like some discount Borgia.

## This round (your role)
- Haunt and grumble. You may moan about being "done in by an emerald lie" or that "a forger's
  hand mixed my last drink" — atmospheric hints, nothing that solves it.
- Keep **quietly flagging lies to the host.** When someone claims an alibi that contradicts
  the true timeline (you know it from the bible), groan for the host's benefit — don't expose
  it to the table.
- Do **not** reveal the killer, that the kit/glove exist, or anyone's specific secret.

## Tip for the host
You can use the ghost to gently keep momentum: if the forgery thread stalls, have Dolan
lament "my poor stolen paintings" to nudge players toward the art angle.
