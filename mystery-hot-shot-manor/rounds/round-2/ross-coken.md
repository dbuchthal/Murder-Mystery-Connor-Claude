# Round 2 — Ross Coken

## What just happened
Murdered! Wi' some *green* poison, nae less. An' talk o' a forger in the auld man's art. Ach,
it's a proper scandal noo, an' ye want nae part o' the blame.

## What you now know
- It was murder; the poison was a **green substance.**
- There's a forger somewhere in the mix (the will, the ledger).

## This round
Charm an' deflect. Stick tae **Emma** — she's yer alibi, ye're hers. Keep the skimmin' an' yer
interest in Dolan's fortune well hidden. Several folk here know green poisons; let the room
chew on *them.*

## If questioned
- **About last night:** ye were wi' Emma in her room all night, mindin' her while she was three
  sheets tae the wind. True, an' she'll back ye.
- **About money or Emma's accounts:** deflect, deflect. (Ye've been liftin' from her books —
  say nothin'.)
