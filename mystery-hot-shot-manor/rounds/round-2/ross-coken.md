# Round 2 — Ross Coken

## What just happened
Murdered! Wi' green paint, nae less — "Paris Green," says the valet. An' talk o' a forger in
the auld man's art. Ach, it's a proper scandal noo, an' ye want nae part o' the blame.

## What you now know
- Poison — an artist's pigment.
- There's a forger somewhere in the mix (the will, the ledger).
- Ye ken nothin' o' paint — ye were a contortionist, no' a painter.

## This round
Charm an' deflect. Stick tae **Emma** — she's yer alibi, ye're hers. Keep the skimmin' an'
yer interest in Dolan's fortune well hidden. If folk fret aboot money motives, steer it
elsewhere.

## If pressed
Ye were wi' Emma in her room all last night, mindin' her while she was three sheets tae the
wind. True, an' she'll back ye. Say nothin' aboot the money ye've been liftin' from her
accounts.
