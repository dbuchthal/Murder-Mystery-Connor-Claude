# Round 2 — Ross Coken

## What just happened
Murdered! Wi' some *green* poison, nae less. An' talk o' a forger who repainted the auld man's
art. Ach, it's a proper scandal noo — an' worse, Buck's got everyone ownin' up tae what poisons
they keep, which is awkward for a man wi' a carnie kit.

- It was murder; the poison was a **green substance.**
- There's a forger somewhere in the mix (the will, the ledger).

## This round

poisons; let the room chew on *them.*

  moonshine floatin' aboot, after all.

  Knowin' it isnae usin' it, aye?
