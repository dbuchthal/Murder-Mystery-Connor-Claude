# Round 1 — Christopher Walken

## What just happened
Dolan is... dead. The bridge is... gone. And you're trapped. In a manor. Full of liars.
*Wow.* Your friend — your *investment* — slumped over a puzzle box. The drama writes itself.

## What you now know
The will is locked in the cryptex, and you need to... work together... to solve it. You'd like 
to know what it says — and you'd like, very much, for nobody to mention that you quietly pulled 
your money out of Dolan's research. Weeks ago. Before all... this.

## This round
Hold court. Perform. Help open the cryptex with theatrical flair. Remind everyone you were in
the parlor *all evening* — many witnesses. Keep the talk away from your finances and the name
"Chappman."

## If pressed

whole time (Contessa, Buck, the staff). Solid. Say nothing of the withdrawn investment.
