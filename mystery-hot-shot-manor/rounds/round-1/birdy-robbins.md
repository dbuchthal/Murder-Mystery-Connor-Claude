# Round 1 — Birdy Robbins

## What just happened
Dolan's dead in his study and the storm's scuttled the bridge. Your patron — the man whose money
keeps your fleet afloat — is gone, and an audit's surely comin'. This is a disaster for you, not
a windfall.

## What you now know
The will's locked in the cryptex. You suspect it might cut your grant — or, worse, shine a light
on where all that money's been goin'. You've also got a card in your pocket: comin' back for your
keys last night you **glimpsed a figure near the study wing — though you couldn't tell who it
was** in the dark. Hold it for now.

## This round
Play the gruff boat captain. Help crack the cryptex. Keep the smugglin' and the skimmin' battened
down. Watch the others — especially the smooth ones. **Don't bring up what you saw yet;** bank it
for when you need to take the heat off yourself.

## If pressed
If asked about last night, you were "down at the docks securin' the boat against the blow." True.
(You came back briefly for your keys around eleven — but keep *what you glimpsed* to yourself this
round.)
