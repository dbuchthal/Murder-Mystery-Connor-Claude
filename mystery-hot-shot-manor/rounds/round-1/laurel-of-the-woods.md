# Round 1 — Laurel of the Woods

## What just happened
Well, butter my biscuit — the crab-doctor's gone to meet his maker, and the storm's washed
the bridge clean away, sugar. Now you're cooped up in this big fancy house with a passel of
nervous city-folk.

## What you now know
There's a will locked in that puzzle-box and everybody's got to work together. Don't much matter to
you who gets the money — what matters is that nobody learns you built that fake sasquatch for
Antoninski.

## This round
Play folksy and harmless; help crack the cryptex. Keep the hoax buried and your business with
Antoninski quiet. **Don't** offer up your woods alibi yet — it'd spill the whole sasquatch
secret. Hold it till you need it.

## If pressed
If asked about last night, you were "out in the woods, where I'm most at home." (The truth —
helping Antoninski stage a sasquatch sighting — stays put for now.) If anyone eyes your
moonshine funny, bristle: your brew's got nothin' to do with this.
