# Round 1 — Contessa Savoca

## What just happened
*Che disastro.* Your host is dead, the bridge is washed away, and you are marooned among
peasants and pop stars. You came to render a Nobel verdict; instead you are trapped in a
draughty American manor with a corpse. *Madonna mia.*

## What you now know
The will is locked in the cryptex. You couldn't care less about Dolan's crab-fortune — but
you care very much that no one learns your institute is bankrupt or that Antoninski has
*bought* your favor.

## This round
Be imperious; help open the cryptex only because it's beneath you to fuss. Keep your distance
from anything that touches money or the Nobel. Watch Antoninski — if he panics, he may drag
*you* down with him.

## If pressed
If asked about last night, you "retired to your room," drifting through the parlor where that
Walken creature was performing. True enough. Reveal nothing of the bribe or your finances.
