# Round 1 — Buck Shanty

## What just happened
You found him. Seven a.m., draped over that cryptex like a man who'd fallen asleep countin'
his sins. You locked the study, gathered the swells, and now you're the closest thing to law
this rock's got. The dame upstairs, the Soviet, the fish lady — all of 'em with something to
hide. Same as always.

## What you now know
You brought Dolan his buttermilk nightcap at **10 o'clock sharp** last night, and it was
**clean** when you set it down — remember that, it'll matter. You also know Dolan secretly
hired you to dig up dirt on these guests, and your notebook's full of it. But a good
detective doesn't show his hand on the first night.

## This round
Help the group open the cryptex. Observe everyone — who flinches at "thief," who sweats, who
oversells their alibi. **Don't reveal your evidence yet** — the coroner's note (Round 2) is
your cue to start working the case. Tonight, you watch and you remember.

## If pressed
You're the valet and the finder of the body; you've got nothing to hide about last night
(you turned in after delivering the nightcap). If asked, say the nightcap was fine when you
brought it — plant that seed.
