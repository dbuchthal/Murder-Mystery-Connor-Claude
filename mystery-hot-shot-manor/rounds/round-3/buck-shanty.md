# Round 3 — Buck Shanty

## What just happened
You tossed the study and came up nearly empty — a smear of green on the glass, the poison and
nothin' else. No glove, no calling card. But the **ledger** earns its keep: the forger Dolan
caught **repainted his canvases.** A *painting* forger. File that hard.

## What you now know (and can reveal this round)
- The scene points at no one — so this case won't be cracked by a dropped clue. It'll be cracked
  by *who fits.*
- The ledger says the forger works in **paint.** Tonight, make the whole room cough up *what kind*
  of faker each of them is — because most of them are the *wrong* kind.

## How to work it (keep prying, don't announce)
Use your leads to force each confession from the *holder:*
- Lean on **Kristof/Laurel** → the **faked sasquatch** (film, casts).
- Lean on **Ross** → **art-fraud cons** (forged provenance).
- Lean on **Emma** → **fencing forged paintings.**
- Lean on **Walken** → **fake identity** and passing fakes.
- Lean on **Contessa** → **certifying and selling fakes.**
- Lean on **Dave** → **forged financials.**
- Lean on **Birdy** → **smuggling forged art.**
- Lean on **Gwen** → an art-world **restorer** "who could fake a canvas" (she'll keep it vague —
  let her, for now).
Notice the pattern for yourself: film, papers, books, names, certificates, smuggling — but only
one trade actually puts a brush to canvas. Keep that thought in your pocket; you can't prove it
until R4.

## This round
Spread the fraud suspicion across all ten. Confirm Walken's painted-over canvas. Let Birdy's
"figure in the dark" prove a sneak was about without naming anyone. **Do not** name a killer —
you don't have the decisive evidence till the luggage search and the lab word (R4).

## If questioned
You handled the glass, so you're a suspect too. If **Gwen** or **Birdy** calls you out, come
clean: Dolan **hired you to investigate his guests,** you're a retired Baltimore PI, and yeah,
you've **faked a document or two** on the job. Insist the nightcap was clean when you set it down.
