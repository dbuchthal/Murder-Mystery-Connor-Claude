# Round 3 — Dave Buchthal

## What just happened
Sweet sassafras. They found a peach glove at the scene, monogrammed **G.B.** — and your wife
is the Georgia Peach, Gwen Buchthal. Your gut twists. Surely there's an explanation. Surely.

## What you now know
- A monogrammed "G.B." glove was at the murder scene, smeared with the poison.
- Your initials are *also* G.B. ("Dave" notwithstanding — you're a Buchthal too).
- You were on the phone to Atlanta during the murder window, so you can't actually say where
  Gwen was. That's eating at you.

## This round
You're torn between loyalty and dawning dread. Defend Gwen *instinctively* — maybe the glove
was stolen, maybe it's a frame. But you're an honest blusterer at heart: if pressed about the
phone call, you **can't lie** about it. Play the man whose certainty is cracking.

## If pressed
Admit, reluctantly, that you were on the **Atlanta call from 10:30 to 11:20** — so you
*assumed* Gwen was upstairs but **never actually saw her there.** You don't want to believe
it. (You still don't know about her forgery or the murder — you're learning this in real
time, and it's breaking your heart.)
