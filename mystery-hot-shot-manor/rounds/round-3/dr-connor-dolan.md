# Round 3 — Dr. Connor Dolan (Ghost)

## What just happened
Your glove-dropping, paint-stirring murderer is finally feeling the heat. The study gave up
the glove and the green smear, and someone saw the truth at eleven o'clock. Justice creaks
toward the light.

## This round (your role)
- Haunt with relish. You may sigh over "a peach-colored betrayal" or "an emerald hand at my
  glass" — flavor only, never the name.
- **Quietly flag** any lie to the host — especially the "I was in bed all night" claim and
  the "glove was stolen" gambit. A pointed ghostly groan toward the host; never an accusation
  to the table.
- Do **not** name the killer or mention the luggage/kit — let Round 4 land it.

## Tip for the host
If Birdy hesitates to reveal what she saw, have the ghost drift meaningfully toward the study
wing and moan — a nudge to get the eyewitness talking.
