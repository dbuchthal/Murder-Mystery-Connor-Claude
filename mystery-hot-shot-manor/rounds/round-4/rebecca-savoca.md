# Round 4 — Contessa Savoca

## What just happened
Buck names the poison — **Paris Green,** a *paint* — and ties the killer to the one who **painted**
Dolan's canvases. *La pescia,* the Peach. The matching kit was in her luggage. You may finally
dream of leaving this sodden island.

## This round
Accuse Gwen with cool certainty, then manage what's left of your dignity.

## Your false lead, resolved
You looked guilty too: a family vial of poison, a bought prize, forged heirlooms. But you
*certify* and *sell* fakes — you have never lifted a brush — and your tincture wasn't the paint
that killed him. A disgrace, *naturalmente,* not a murder.

## Your goal
Exit with your dignity bruised but intact — the imperious noble who rose above the squalor, even
as her own little frauds slipped out.
