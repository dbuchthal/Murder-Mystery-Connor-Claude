# Round 4 — Contessa Savoca

## What just happened
The poison kit in the Buchthal woman's luggage ends it — *she* is the murderer. *Naturalmente.*
You may finally dream of leaving this sodden island. But Antoninski's exposed fraud has dragged
your own arrangement toward the light.

## This round
Accuse Gwen with cool certainty. Then manage the damage to your own reputation.

## Your secret, resolved
You took a **bribe from Antoninski** and your Savoca Institute is **bankrupt.** A disgrace, but
not a murder. Frame yourself as *deceived* by a faked discovery rather than complicit — protect
the only currency you have left, your name. "I was *misled,* cretini. I render no prize to a
fraud."

## Your goal
Exit with your dignity bruised but intact. The imperious noble who rose above the squalor —
even as her own secret slipped out.
