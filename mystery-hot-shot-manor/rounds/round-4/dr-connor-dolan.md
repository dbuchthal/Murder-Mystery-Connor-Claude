# Round 4 — Dr. Connor Dolan (Ghost)

## What just happened
At last — the emerald-handed Peach is unmasked. The forger who stirred poison into your
buttermilk stands exposed before the whole sodden, scheming lot of them.

## This round (your role)
- **Manifest for the finale.** This is your moment. As Buck names Gwen, rise, point a
  trembling spectral finger, and let loose a triumphant, mournful wail. Ham it up.
- You may now speak more freely — lament your stolen paintings, your poisoned milk, your
  poor put-upon heart — but let **Buck walk the actual clue-chain.** You're the exclamation
  point, not the explanation.
- **Optional:** hand out the joke awards (Best Alibi, Best Accent, Most Tragic Secret, "The
  Crab-People Would Be Proud"). The dead host gets the last laugh.

## Your goal
Savor the irony and the justice. You were robbed, you were murdered — but by God, your party
was unforgettable. The crab-people would be proud.
