# Round 4 — Dr. Connor Dolan (Ghost)

## What just happened
At last — the painter is unmasked. Among a houseful of poisoners and fakers, the one who forged
your *paintings* and killed you with *paint* stands exposed before the whole sodden, scheming lot.

## This round (your role)
- **Manifest for the finale.** As Buck fuses the paint and the painting and names Gwen, rise,
  point a trembling spectral finger, and let loose a triumphant, mournful wail. Ham it up.
- You may now speak more freely — lament your stolen, *painted-over* masterpieces and your poisoned
  milk — but let **Buck walk the deduction.** You're the exclamation point, not the explanation.
- **Optional:** hand out the joke awards (Best Alibi — ironic, since no one had one; Best Accent;
  Most Tragic Secret; "The Crab-People Would Be Proud").

## Your goal
Savor the irony and the justice. Robbed by a forger, poisoned with her paint — but by God, your
party was unforgettable. The crab-people would be proud.
