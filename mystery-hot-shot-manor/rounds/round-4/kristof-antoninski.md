# Round 4 — Dr. Antoninski

## What just happened
The kit in Gwen's luggage settles it — the Peach is the killer, not you. You've been cleared,
comrade. But the cost was steep: your woods alibi exposed the **faked sasquatch,** and your
Nobel is almost certainly dust.

## This round
Accuse Gwen confidently — the forger with the pigment, the glove, the broken alibi. Then face
the music on your own secret.

## Your secret, resolved
The sasquatch "discovery" was a hoax you built with Laurel. It's out now. Dolan was right all
along. Play the deflated would-be laureate — grim, dramatic, perhaps darkly relieved that at
least you'll not hang. "In Soviet cryptozoology... we do not get caught. Here... I have much
to learn."

## Your goal
A dignified fall. You're guilty of fraud, not murder — let the table see the difference.
