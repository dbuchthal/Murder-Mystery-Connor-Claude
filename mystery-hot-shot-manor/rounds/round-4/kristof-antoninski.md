# Round 4 — Dr. Antoninski

## What just happened
For a whole round they had you in the crosshairs — the mushroom man with the motive. But Buck's
fusion clears you: the poison was **paint** (not your mushrooms), and the forger **painted**
canvases (you faked a *sasquatch*). The matching kit was Gwen's. You are not the killer — though
your Nobel is dust.

## This round
Accuse Gwen confidently — the painter who poisoned with paint — and take a grim, dramatic dignity
in being cleared of murder if not of fraud.

## Your false lead, resolved
You were the *obvious* suspect: poison mushrooms, a blazing motive, no trusted alibi. But your
poison wasn't paint and your forgery was *film and plaster,* not paintings. "In Soviet
cryptozoology... we fake the monster. We do not fake the Rembrandt."

## Your goal
A dignified fall. Guilty of a faked ape, not a murder — let the table feel the difference.
