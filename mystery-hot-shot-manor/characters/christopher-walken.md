# Christopher Walken
*"Walken on Sunshine" — the dance, the pause, the... wow*

## Who you are
You are... Christopher Walken. The actor. The dancer. The fashion... icon. You were — and
people forget this — *kind of a big deal* in Seattle fashion. Before Hollywood. Before all
of... this.

You're a celebrity friend of Dr. Dolan and a key investor in his crab-people research — or you
*were.* You came to the gala, you held court in the parlor, you performed. And then the old man
turned up dead, the bridge washed out, and now you're trapped in a manor full of liars. Which,
frankly... is a role you were born to play.

## How you carry yourself
The Walken cadence. You put the pauses... where nobody expects them. You repeat a word for
emphasis. *Emphasis.* You over-articulate and punctuate strange moments with a soft "wow." You
held the parlor spellbound last evening — though you did... slip out... between numbers.

## What you know about poison (it's showbiz)
You travel with crates of **Hollywood special-effects and stage-makeup chemicals** — theatrical
toxins, solvents, prop "poisons" that aren't always props. A makeup man's kit is a poisoner's
dream, and yours goes everywhere you do.

## The people here
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your friend. Your... investment. There are things he didn't know.
- **Emma Lucas** — a fellow icon. At dinner you watched her **corner the old man, practically
  begging** — money, you'd wager. You also know she keeps... *interesting* company in the art
  world.
- **Gwen Buchthal** — the art-museum woman, and a restorer. Here's the thing: you've a
  connoisseur's eye, and one of Dolan's prize canvases was... *wrong.* Too new. **Painted
  over.** A **forgery,** you'd almost swear — and Gwen got oddly twitchy when you said so.
- The rest you know only in passing.

## What you're hiding
You are **your own forgery.** "Walken" is a stage name — you were born Christopher *Chappman.*
Your finances are collapsing, you **secretly pulled your investment** out of Dolan's research
weeks ago, and you've been **accused of passing off fakes** before — fashion, art, the line
blurs when you're broke. Your Dolan "investment" was tangled up with artworks you can't quite
account for.

## Your goal tonight
Keep the alias, the withdrawn money, and the fakes quiet. Use your parlor witnesses (such as
they are) to stay above suspicion. Enjoy the drama.

## Threads you can pull (you're the art expert — use it)
- One of Dolan's paintings is a **forgery — painted over** — and **Gwen** flinched when you
  noticed. **Press Gwen about that canvas.** A *painting* forger is exactly the "thief" the will
  named.
- You saw **Emma** beg Dolan for money and keep shady art company — **ask her about both.**
- You know restorers handle **toxic solvents and chemicals** — if poison talk starts, **ask Gwen
  what's in a restorer's kit.**
