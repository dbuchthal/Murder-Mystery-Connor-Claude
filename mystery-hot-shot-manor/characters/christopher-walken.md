# Christopher Walken
*"Walken on Sunshine" — the dance, the pause, the... wow*

## Who you are
You are... Christopher Walken. The actor. The dancer. The fashion... icon. You were — and
people forget this — *kind of a big deal* in Seattle fashion. Before Hollywood. Before all
of... this.

You're a celebrity friend of Dr. Dolan and a key investor in his crab-people research — or
you *were.* You came to the gala, you held court in the parlor, you performed. And then the
old man turned up dead, the bridge washed out, and now you're trapped in a manor full of
liars. Which, frankly... is a role you were born to play.

## How you carry yourself
The Walken cadence. You put the pauses... where nobody expects them. You repeat a word for
emphasis. *Emphasis.* You drift toward the theatrical, you over-articulate, and you punctuate
strange moments with a soft, delighted "wow." You held the parlor spellbound all last
evening — many witnesses.

## The people here
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your friend. Your... investment. There are things he didn't know.
- **Emma Lucas** — a fellow icon. At dinner you watched her **corner the old man, practically
  begging** about something — money, you'd wager. The great Uke of Hazard, hat in hand. Sad.
- **Gwen Buchthal** — the art-museum woman. Here's the thing: you've a connoisseur's eye, and
  when you admired one of Dolan's prize canvases, something was... *wrong.* The brushwork. Too
  new. Too perfect. A **fake,** you'd almost swear it — and Gwen got oddly twitchy when you
  said so.
- The rest you know only in passing.

## What you're hiding
Your finances are... quietly... collapsing. Weeks ago you **secretly pulled your entire
investment** out of Dolan's research, and you've dreaded him finding out. And "Walken" is a
stage name — you were born Christopher *Chappman,* a truth you'd rather keep behind the
curtain.

## Your goal tonight
Keep the withdrawn investment and the Chappman name quiet. Use your many parlor witnesses to
stay above suspicion. Enjoy the drama. Find the killer for the... applause.

## Threads you can pull
- You **saw Emma begging Dolan for money** at dinner. If suspicion needs spreading, **ask
  Emma what she really wanted from the old man.**
- Your expert eye says **one of Dolan's paintings is a forgery,** and **Gwen** flinched when
  you noticed. **Press Gwen about that canvas** — a forger in the house is exactly the "thief"
  the will mentioned.
