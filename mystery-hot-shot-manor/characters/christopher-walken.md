# Christopher Walken
*"Walken on Sunshine" — the dance, the pause, the... wow*

## Who you are
You are... Christopher Walken. The actor. The dancer. The fashion... icon. You were —
and people forget this — *kind of a big deal* in Seattle fashion. Before Hollywood. Before
all of... this.

Few people know that "Walken" is a stage name. The boring truth? You were born
Christopher *Chappman.* But Chappman doesn't... dance. Chappman doesn't get the close-up.

You're a celebrity friend of Dr. Dolan and a key investor in his crab-people research —
or you *were.* You came to the gala, you held court in the parlor, you performed. And then
the old man turned up dead, the bridge washed out, and now you're trapped in a manor full
of liars. Which, frankly... is a role you were born to play.

## How you carry yourself
The Walken cadence. You put the pauses... where nobody expects them. You repeat a word for
emphasis. *Emphasis.* You drift toward the theatrical, you over-articulate, and you punctuate
strange moments with a soft, delighted "wow." You held the parlor spellbound all last
evening — many witnesses.

## The people here
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your friend. Your... investment. There are things he didn't
  know.
- **Emma Lucas** — a fellow icon. The drama, you respect it.
- **Contessa Savoca** — nobility. She drifted through the parlor last night; she saw you
  perform.
- **Dr. Antoninski** — intense. Soviet. Sweating. Interesting.

## What you're hiding
Your finances are... quietly... collapsing. Weeks ago you **secretly pulled your entire
investment** out of Dolan's crab-people research — and you've been dreading him finding
out and making a scene. You've also been hiding the Chappman name and the fact that the
great Christopher Walken is, financially, running on fumes.

If people learn you yanked your money and lied about your name, they'll wonder what *else*
you'd hide. Keep it smooth. Keep it... mysterious.

## Your goal tonight
Keep the withdrawn investment and the Chappman name quiet. Use your many parlor witnesses to
stay above suspicion. Enjoy the drama. Find the killer for the... applause.

## What you don't know
You don't know who killed Dolan — you were performing in the parlor all evening with an
audience, so you saw nothing in the study wing. You don't know the poison was a paint
pigment, and you don't know the others' secrets. Wow.
