# Laurel of the Woods
*"The Moonshine Mama" — big jugs of brew and a head full of backwoods secrets*

## Who you are
Folks call you Laurel of the Woods, sugar, and there ain't a holler, roadhouse, or back-
country bar in these parts that don't light up when you roll in with your jugs of **potent
moonshine.** You were born to these forests; you know every deer trail, every creek, every
place a body could hide. That's how you caught the eye of that Soviet fella, **Dr.
Antoninski**, who hired you on as his guide and tracker to hunt the sasquatch.

Now the old crab-doctor's gone and died up at the big house, the storm's washed out the
bridge, and you're stuck inside with a passel of city-folk who wrinkle their noses at your
brew. Pity 'em, sugar. They don't know what they're missin'.

## How you carry yourself
Twangy, folksy, sly as a fox. You sprinkle in hill-country proverbs ("that dog won't hunt,"
"slicker'n owl grease") and call everybody "sugar" or "city-folk." You play dumb, but you
miss *nothing.*

## The people here
- **Dr. Antoninski** — your employer. You were out in the woods with him last night doing a
  *particular* job (see below). You watch his back; he pays well.
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — the other science fella, with his crabs. Didn't know him well.
- **Buck Shanty** — the valet's got lawman eyes. You've dodged enough revenuers to know the
  type.
- **Contessa Savoca** — fancy foreign lady. The whole woods job last night was a show put on
  for *her.*

## What you're hiding
Here's the big one, sugar: **you built the fake sasquatch.** You sewed the suit, you cast the
plaster footprints, you faked the howls — the whole hoax, for Antoninski's good money. The
"discovery" that's about to win him a Nobel is *your handiwork,* and it's a lie from root to
branch.

And one more thing folk might point at: your **moonshine** is strong enough to drop a moose.
If anyone thinks Dolan was poisoned with *drink,* they'll look square at you. (Spoiler for
your peace of mind: it wasn't your brew — but you don't know that yet, and you'll be nervous.)

## Your alibi (and Antoninski's)
From about **10:15 to 11:30 PM** last night, you and Antoninski snuck out into the woods to
**stage a fresh sasquatch "sighting"** for the Contessa — fresh footprints, a howl or two on
the night air. You were together the whole time. You can clear him — but only by admitting
the hoax.

## Your goal tonight
Keep the fake-sasquatch racket buried (your livelihood and Antoninski's Nobel depend on it).
Defend your moonshine's good name. Decide when to use the woods alibi. Don't get pinned for a
killin' you didn't do.

## What you don't know
You don't know who killed Dolan. You were in the woods with Antoninski, so you saw nothing in
the house. You don't yet know the poison was an **artist's pigment** (not booze) — once that
comes out, your moonshine's in the clear. You don't know the others' secrets.
