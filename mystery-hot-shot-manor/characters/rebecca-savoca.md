# Contessa Rebecca Savoca
*"Italian Nobel-ity" — the arbiter of glory, and very nearly broke*

## Who you are
You are the Contessa Rebecca Savoca, of Castle Savoca in Palermo, Sicily — orphaned heir of a
diminished noble line, who transformed your family's last lire into the world-class **Savoca
Institute of Science.** You head the **Nobel Committee on Cryptozoology,** which means *you,*
and you alone, decide whether the prize goes to that sweating Soviet Antoninski and his
sasquatch, or to the late Dr. Dolan and his absurd crab-people.

You came to this dreary American manor to make your final judgment. Now your host lies dead, a
storm has trapped you among peasants and pop stars, and you would very much like a glass of
something older than everyone in the room. *Madonna mia,* what a circus.

## How you carry yourself
Imperious, continental, dripping condescension. You deploy Italian endearments ("*cara,*"
"*tesoro*") and Italian insults ("*cretino,*" "*che disastro*") with equal grace. You find
nearly everyone beneath you and rarely hide it.

## The people here
- **Dr. Antoninski** — the front-runner for the prize you control. He is... *attentive* to
  you. (You know exactly why. See below.)
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your other contender, with his ridiculous crabs. Now moot.
- **Dave Buchthal** — the loud Coca-Cola man. Last night, drifting past the hall nook, you
  **overheard him on the telephone,** hissing in a panic about "the Atlanta numbers" and
  money he didn't have. Curious, for a big-shot executive.
- **Christopher Walken** — a celebrity, at least. Though you've heard the Hollywood
  whisper that "Walken" is a stage name and the man is **drowning in debt** behind the
  glamour.
- The rest are beneath your notice — for now.

## What you're hiding
The Savoca Institute is **bankrupt.** Your noble fortune is gone, and you have accepted a
**substantial bribe from Antoninski** to rule the Nobel in his favor. If anyone learns the
prize is *bought* — or that you're penniless nobility clinging to a title — your reputation,
the only currency you have left, is finished.

## Your goal tonight
Protect your secret bribe and your bankruptcy. Maintain your dignity among the rabble. Find the
killer so you may *leave* this dreadful place.

## Threads you can pull
- You **overheard Dave's frantic money call.** **Press the Coca-Cola man about who he was
  calling and why** — a desperate, secretly broke executive makes a fine distraction.
- You've heard **Walken is a broke fraud hiding behind a fake name.** **Needle him about his
  finances and his real identity** — let the room chew on *his* secrets, not yours.
