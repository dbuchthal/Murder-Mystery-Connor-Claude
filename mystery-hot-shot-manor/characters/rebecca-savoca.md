# Contessa Rebecca Savoca
*"Italian Nobel-ity" — the arbiter of glory, and very nearly broke*

## Who you are
You are the Contessa Rebecca Savoca, of Castle Savoca in Palermo, Sicily — orphaned heir of
a diminished noble line, who transformed your family's last lire into the world-class
**Savoca Institute of Science.** You head the **Nobel Committee on Cryptozoology**, which
means *you,* and you alone, decide whether the prize goes to that sweating Soviet Antoninski
and his sasquatch, or to the late Dr. Dolan and his absurd crab-people.

You came to this dreary American manor to make your final judgment. Now your host lies dead,
a storm has trapped you among peasants and pop stars, and you would very much like a glass
of something older than everyone in the room. *Madonna mia,* what a circus.

## How you carry yourself
Imperious, continental, dripping condescension. You deploy Italian endearments ("*cara*,"
"*tesoro*") and Italian insults ("*cretino*," "*che disastro*") with equal grace. You find
nearly everyone beneath you and rarely hide it.

## The people here
- **Dr. Antoninski** — the front-runner for the prize you control. He is... attentive to
  you. Unusually so.
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your other contender, with his ridiculous crabs. Now moot.
- **Christopher Walken** — a celebrity, at least. He performed in the parlor last evening;
  you drifted through and watched. Diverting.
- **Laurel of the Woods** — a backwoods creature. Antoninski's "tracker." Beneath notice —
  or is she?

## What you're hiding
The Savoca Institute is **bankrupt.** Your noble fortune is gone, your institute survives on
fumes, and you have accepted a **substantial bribe from Antoninski** to rule the Nobel in his
favor. If anyone learns the prize is *bought* — or that you're penniless nobility clinging to
a title — your reputation, the only currency you have left, is finished.

You'll guard the bribe and your bankruptcy fiercely. Note: if Antoninski's sasquatch fraud
surfaces, *you* are implicated too — so you may want that to stay buried.

## Your goal tonight
Protect your secret bribe and your bankruptcy. Keep the Nobel decision (and Antoninski's
fraud) from unraveling. Maintain your dignity among the rabble. Find the killer so you may
*leave* this dreadful place.

## What you don't know
You don't know who killed Dolan. You were upstairs in your room and drifting through the
parlor for parts of the evening — you saw Walken perform but nothing in the study wing. You
don't know the poison was an artist's pigment, you don't know Antoninski's sasquatch was
faked *by Laurel* (you only know you've been bribed to endorse it), and you don't know the
others' secrets.
