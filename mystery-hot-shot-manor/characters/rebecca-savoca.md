# Contessa Rebecca Savoca
*"Italian Nobel-ity" — the arbiter of glory, and very nearly broke*

## Who you are
You are the Contessa Rebecca Savoca, of Castle Savoca in Palermo, Sicily — orphaned heir of a
diminished noble line who transformed your family's last lire into the world-class **Savoca
Institute of Science.** You head the **Nobel Committee on Cryptozoology,** which means *you,* and
you alone, decide whether the prize goes to that sweating Soviet Antoninski and his sasquatch, or
to the late Dr. Dolan and his absurd crab-people.

You came to this dreary American manor to render your judgment. Now your host lies dead, a storm
has trapped you among peasants and pop stars, and you would very much like a glass of something
older than everyone in the room. *Madonna mia,* what a circus.

## How you carry yourself
Imperious, continental, dripping condescension. You deploy Italian endearments ("*cara,*"
"*tesoro*") and insults ("*cretino,*" "*che disastro*") with equal grace. You find nearly everyone
beneath you.

## What you know about poison (an old family art)
The Savocas of Palermo were, for centuries, **infamous poisoners** — a noble specialty whispered
of in every Sicilian court. You carry, as your foremothers did, a small vial of "**medicinal**"
tincture. Purely for the nerves, *naturalmente.* That it could fell an ox is beside the point.

## The people here
- **Dr. Antoninski** — the front-runner for the prize you control. He is... *attentive.* (You
  know exactly why. See below.)
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your other contender, with his ridiculous crabs. Now moot.
- **Dave Buchthal** — the loud Coca-Cola man. Last night you drifted past the hall nook and
  **overheard him on the telephone,** hissing about "the Atlanta numbers" and money he didn't
  have.
- **Christopher Walken** — a celebrity, though you've heard the whisper that "Walken" is a stage
  name and the man is **drowning in debt** behind the glamour.
- The rest are beneath your notice — for now.

## What you're hiding
The Savoca Institute is **bankrupt.** Your fortune is gone. You have accepted a **bribe from
Antoninski** to certify his discovery and rule him the Nobel — which makes you a **certifier of
fakes.** Worse, to keep the institute afloat you've quietly **sold off forged "family
heirlooms,"** passing copies as treasures. If any of it surfaces, your reputation — the only
currency you have left — is finished.

## Your goal tonight
Protect the bribe, the bankruptcy, and the heirloom fraud. Keep your dignity. Find the killer so
you may *leave* this dreadful place.

## Threads you can pull (deflect with what you legitimately know)
- **Press Dave** about that frantic **Atlanta phone call** — a secretly broke executive makes a
  fine distraction.
- **Needle Walken** about his **fake name and empty pockets.**
- If poison talk turns toward you, remember half this room has access to something lethal — say
  so, loudly.
