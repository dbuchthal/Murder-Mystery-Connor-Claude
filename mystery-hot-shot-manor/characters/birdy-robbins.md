# Ms. Jay "Birdy" Robbins
*"The Canary in the Coal Mine" — salt in her hair, secrets in her hold*

## Who you are
Name's Birdy Robbins, and you've got more sea miles than the rest of these powder-puffs have
hot baths. You own the **Red Irish Lord fishery** out in the San Juans, and for years Dr.
Dolan's been payin' you to run "research expeditions" — haulin' him out to look for his
precious crab-people. The man funneled a *boatload* of grant money your way.

Now he's dead at his desk, the storm's washed out the bridge, and you're stuck in this drafty
manor with a bunch of landlubbers who couldn't tie a bowline if their lives depended on it.
You came up to the docks last night to secure your vessel before the blow. You saw a thing or
two on your way back in. Maybe you'll mention it. Maybe you won't.

## How you carry yourself
Gruff, nautical, deadpan. You start to curse like a sailor, then catch yourself in polite
company. Everything's a boat metaphor. You don't suffer fools, and this room is *full* of 'em.

## What you know about poison (it's just part of the job)
Out on the water, your **anchor chain hauls up toxic green kelp** — a slimy bloom that'll
blister your hands and stop a heart if you're fool enough to brew it. You handle it careful and
dump it overboard. You know that green slime is *deadly,* and you know more about marine
poisons than anyone in this house of perfumed ninnies.

## The people here
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your patron and meal ticket. You needed that man *alive.*
- **Buck Shanty** — the valet. He's got **ex-cop eyes,** and he watches every soul in this
  house like he's takin' notes. You've dodged enough revenuers to know the type — that man is
  no ordinary manservant.
- **Gwen Buchthal** — the Peach. Funny... you saw her where she shouldn't've been last night.
  (See below.)
- The rest are strangers from the mainland.

## What you're hiding
The "research fleet" is partly a **smugglin' operation,** and you've been **skimmin' Dolan's
grant money** for years. The IRS is already circlin' your wake. An audit would sink you — which
is exactly why you needed Dolan **alive** to keep the money and the cover story flowin'.

## What you saw (your big card)
Last night you went down to secure your boat, then came back for your **forgotten keys** around
**11:00 PM.** On your way through the hall you **saw Gwen Buchthal hurrying out of the study
wing** — not headed upstairs to bed, but *away from Dolan's study.* You also half-noticed
**something peach-colored dropped on the floor.** You weren't sure it meant anything... until
now. Decide when (and to whom) to play this card.

## Your goal tonight
Keep the smuggling/skimming battened down. Decide when to reveal what you saw — it takes the
heat off you and points it at Gwen. Don't get pinned for a killin' that *ruins* you.

## A thread you can pull
That valet, **Buck,** is no valet. **Call him out on those lawman eyes** — ask him straight
what he's really doin' in this house. Better the room wonders about *him* than about your
ledgers.
