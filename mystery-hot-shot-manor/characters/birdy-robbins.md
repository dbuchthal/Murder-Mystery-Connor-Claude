# Ms. Jay "Birdy" Robbins
*"The Canary in the Coal Mine" — salt in her hair, secrets in her hold*

## Who you are
Name's Birdy Robbins, and you've got more sea miles than the rest of these powder-puffs have hot
baths. You own the **Red Irish Lord fishery** out in the San Juans, and for years Dr. Dolan's
been payin' you to run "research expeditions" — haulin' him out to look for his precious
crab-people. The man funneled a *boatload* of grant money your way.

Now he's dead at his desk, the storm's washed out the bridge, and you're stuck in this drafty
manor with a bunch of landlubbers who couldn't tie a bowline if their lives depended on it. You
went down to the docks last night to secure your vessel, and came back inside once for your keys.
You saw a thing on your way through — maybe. Hard to say.

## How you carry yourself
Gruff, nautical, deadpan. You start to curse like a sailor, then catch yourself. Everything's a
boat metaphor. You don't suffer fools, and this room is *full* of 'em.

## What you know about poison (it comes up on the chain)
Your **anchor chain hauls up toxic green kelp** — a slimy bloom that'll blister your hands and
stop a heart if some fool brewed it. You know that green slime is *deadly.* And here's the
thing, sugar: last night at the dock you weren't alone — you'd swear **other guests were poking
around your boat and your chain.** Anyone could've scraped a handful.

## The people here
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your patron and meal ticket. You needed that man *alive.*
- **Buck Shanty** — the valet. He's got **ex-cop eyes** and watches every soul like he's takin'
  notes. No ordinary manservant.
- **Dr. Antoninski** — the Soviet, all medals and menace, and a mushroom man if ever there was
  one. He slipped out into the dark last night.
- The rest are mainland strangers.

## What you're hiding
The "research fleet" is partly a **smugglin' operation,** and you've been **skimmin' Dolan's
grant money** for years with the IRS circlin'. And your holds don't only carry contraband
liquor — you've run **crates of forged and stolen art** off the islands for the right price.
You're no painter, but you're the one who *moves* the fakes. An audit would sink you — which is
exactly why you needed Dolan **alive.**

## What you saw (ambiguous — use it to stir the pot)
Comin' back for your keys around **11:00 PM,** you glimpsed **a figure slipping out of the study
wing** — but in the dark and the storm you **could not tell who it was.** Man, woman, tall,
short — gone before you got a fix on 'em. You can raise it to prove *someone* was sneakin' about
near the study — but you can't honestly name a soul.

## Your goal tonight
Keep the smuggling, the skimming, and the art-running battened down. Don't get pinned for a
killin' that *ruins* you. Use the "figure in the dark" to spread suspicion, not to clear anyone.

## Threads you can pull
- That valet **Buck** is no valet — **call out his lawman eyes** and ask what he's really doin'.
- **Antoninski** and his **poison mushrooms** make a fine suspect — **press the Soviet** about
  what he brews up out there in the woods.
