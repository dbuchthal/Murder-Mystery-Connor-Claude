# Ms. Jay "Birdy" Robbins
*"The Canary in the Coal Mine" — salt in her hair, secrets in her hold*

## Who you are
Name's Birdy Robbins, and you've got more sea miles than the rest of these powder-puffs
have hot baths. You own the **Red Irish Lord fishery** out in the San Juans, and for years
Dr. Dolan's been payin' you to run "research expeditions" — haulin' him out to look for his
precious crab-people. The man funneled a *boatload* of grant money your way, bless his
barnacled heart.

Now he's dead at his desk, the storm's washed out the bridge, and you're stuck in this
draughty manor with a bunch of landlubbers who couldn't tie a bowline if their lives
depended on it. You came up to the docks last night to secure your vessel before the blow.
You saw a thing or two on your way back in. Maybe you'll mention it. Maybe you won't.

## How you carry yourself
Gruff, nautical, deadpan. You start to curse like a sailor, then catch yourself in polite
company. Everything's a boat metaphor. You don't suffer fools, and this room is *full* of
'em.

## The people here
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your patron and meal ticket. You needed that man *alive.*
- **Buck Shanty** — the valet. Ex-cop eyes. You don't trust a man who watches that close.
- **Gwen Buchthal** — the Peach. Funny... you saw her where she shouldn't've been last
  night. (See below.)
- **Dr. Antoninski** — the Soviet. All medals and menace. Slipped out into the woods at
  some point.

## What you're hiding
The "research fleet" is partly a **smugglin' operation**, and you've been **skimmin'
Dolan's grant money** for years. The IRS is already circlin' your wake. An audit would sink
you to the bottom — which is exactly why you needed Dolan **alive** to keep the money and
the cover story flowin'. His death is the worst thing that could've happened to you.

You'll keep the smuggling and the skimming battened down. If folk learn the IRS is after
you, they'll think you silenced Dolan — but his death *exposes* you, it doesn't save you.

## What you saw (your big card)
Last night you went down to secure your boat, then came back for your **forgotten keys**
around **11:00 PM.** On your way through the hall you **saw Gwen Buchthal hurrying out of
the study wing** — not headed upstairs to bed like she'd claim, but *away from Dolan's
study.* You also half-noticed **something peach-colored dropped on the floor.** You weren't
sure it meant anything... until now.

You don't have to volunteer this right away — but if someone presses you about the study
wing, or about Gwen's whereabouts, this is yours to reveal.

## Your goal tonight
Keep the smuggling/skimming hidden. Decide when (and to whom) to reveal what you saw — it
takes the heat off you and points it at Gwen. Don't get pinned for a murder that *ruins*
you.

## What you don't know
You don't know *why* Gwen was there or that she's the killer/forger — you just know what
you saw. You don't know the poison was an artist's pigment. You don't know the others'
secrets.
