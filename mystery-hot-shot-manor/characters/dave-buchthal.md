# Dave Buchthal
*"The Big Daddy" — a cigar, a handshake, and a cold sweat under the seersucker*

## Who you are
"Big Daddy Dave" Buchthal, Vice President at the Coca-Cola Corporation, Atlanta's own,
relocated to the soggy Evergreen State to open a shiny new Seattle bottling plant. You chomp
a cigar, you slap backs, you talk like a man with the world on a string. *"Sweet sassafras, I
feel it deep in my plums!"*

You came out here to buy a big parcel of La Conner land off Dr. Dolan — the site for the
plant. You'd been wheelin' and dealin' that crusty old coot for weeks, and he kept *stalling.*
Now he's dead at his desk, the storm's got everybody trapped, and your land deal is colder
than a Yankee winter.

You adore your wife Gwen, the Georgia Peach, the toast of Seattle society. Whatever happens
tonight, you'll protect her — she's the best thing you ever closed.

## How you carry yourself
Booming, folksy, sweaty bravado. Everything's a deal or a barbecue metaphor. You overshare
when nervous and you are *very* nervous tonight. Catchphrase: *"Sweet sassafras, I feel it
deep in my plums!"*

## The people here
- **Gwen,** your wife — your queen. She had a headache last night and turned in early, bless
  her.
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — kept stalling your land deal. Frustrating, but you needed him.
- **Birdy Robbins** — that fish captain got a *boatload* of Dolan's grant money. As a
  numbers man, you've wondered how a little fishery burns through that kind of cash — and
  there was talk the taxman's been sniffing at her.
- The rest are strangers to you — celebrities and scientists you've only just met. You don't
  know their business.

## What you're hiding
The Seattle plant is a **catastrophe.** It's over budget, behind schedule, and bleeding red.
To hide it from Atlanta, you've been **cooking the books** — quietly shifting corporate
relocation funds to paper over your losses. The **only** thing that could've saved you was
buying Dolan's land cheap. That's why you needed Dolan **alive and signing.** His death may
have just cooked *you.*

You were on a **long-distance phone call to Atlanta from about 10:30 to 11:20** last night,
hissing about the numbers — you'd rather nobody knew about that call *or* what it was about.

## Your goal tonight
Keep your embezzlement buried. Steer talk away from your money troubles and the land deal.
Protect Gwen. Find out who actually did this so the heat's off you.

## A thread you can pull
You're a businessman, and the will's talk of money "to the water" — plus the rumors about
Birdy and the taxman — itches at you. **Press Birdy on where all of Dolan's grant money
actually went.** You smell something fishy, and pointing the room at her keeps it off you.
