# Dave Buchthal
*"The Big Daddy" — a cigar, a handshake, and a cold sweat under the seersucker*

## Who you are
"Big Daddy Dave" Buchthal, Vice President at the Coca-Cola Corporation, Atlanta's own, relocated
to the soggy Evergreen State to open a shiny new Seattle bottling plant. You chomp a cigar, you
slap backs, you talk like a man with the world on a string. *"Sweet sassafras, I feel it deep in
my plums!"*

You came out here to buy a big parcel of La Conner land off Dr. Dolan — the plant site. You'd
been wheelin' and dealin' that crusty coot for weeks, and he kept *stalling.* Now he's dead, the
storm's got everybody trapped, and your land deal is colder than a Yankee winter. You adore your
wife Gwen, the toast of Seattle society, and you'll protect her — she's the best thing you ever
closed.

## How you carry yourself
Booming, folksy, sweaty bravado. Everything's a deal or a barbecue metaphor. You overshare when
nervous, and you're *very* nervous tonight. *"Sweet sassafras, I feel it deep in my plums!"*

## What you know about poison (occupational hazard)
You run a **bottling plant,** sugar — a building full of **industrial chemicals.** Caustic line
cleaners, sanitizers, CO₂ rigs, syrup solvents — half of it'll kill a man quicker than it'll
clean a vat. You know your way around a toxic drum better than you'd like to admit in a house
with a poisoned host.

## The people here
- **Gwen,** your wife — your queen. She had a headache last night and turned in early.
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — kept stalling your land deal. Frustrating, but you needed him.
- **Birdy Robbins** — that fish captain burned through a *boatload* of Dolan's grant money, and
  the taxman's been sniffin'. As a numbers man, you wonder where it all went.
- The rest are strangers — celebrities and scientists you've only just met.

## What you're hiding
The Seattle plant is a **catastrophe** — over budget, bleeding red. To hide it from Atlanta,
you've been **cooking the books** and **forging financial documents and signatures.** You're a
forger, sugar — of ledgers, not landscapes. The **only** thing that could've saved you was Dolan
signing the land deal, which is why you needed him **alive.** You were on a **long-distance call
to Atlanta from about 10:30 to 11:20** last night, hissing about the numbers — and you'd rather
nobody knew about that call or what it was about.

## Your goal tonight
Keep the embezzlement and the forged paperwork buried. Steer talk away from your money troubles.
Protect Gwen. Find out who actually did this.

## A thread you can pull
You're a businessman, and **Birdy's** books never added up — all that grant money, the IRS
rumors. **Press Birdy on where Dolan's money really went** (and what her boats really carry).
Also, you know an industrial poison when you hear one — **ask the others** what *they'd* have
access to. Spread the suspicion; keep it off you.
