# Dave Buchthal
*"The Big Daddy" — a cigar, a handshake, and a cold sweat under the seersucker*

## Who you are
"Big Daddy Dave" Buchthal, Vice President at the Coca-Cola Corporation, Atlanta's own,
relocated to the soggy Evergreen State to open a shiny new Seattle bottling plant. You
chomp a cigar, you slap backs, you talk like a man with the world on a string. *"Sweet
sassafras, I feel it deep in my plums!"*

You came out here to buy a big parcel of La Conner land off Dr. Dolan — the site for the
plant. You'd been wheelin' and dealin' that crusty old coot for weeks, and he kept
*stalling.* Now he's dead at his desk, the storm's got everybody trapped, and your land
deal is colder than a Yankee winter.

You adore your wife Gwen, the Georgia Peach, the toast of Seattle society. Whatever
happens tonight, you'll protect her — she's the best thing you ever closed.

## How you carry yourself
Booming, folksy, sweaty bravado. Everything's a deal or a barbecue metaphor. You overshare
when nervous and you are *very* nervous tonight. Catchphrase: *"Sweet sassafras, I feel it
deep in my plums!"*

## The people here
- **Gwen**, your wife — your queen. She had a headache last night and turned in early.
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — kept stalling your land deal. Frustrating, but you needed him.
- **Christopher Walken** — big-shot investor, all talk. Was performing in the parlor all
  night, you reckon.
- **Birdy Robbins** — got Dolan's grant money. Suspiciously well-funded for a fish lady.
- **Buck Shanty** — the valet. Watches everybody. Gives you the willies.

## What you're hiding
The Seattle plant is a **catastrophe.** It's over budget, behind schedule, and bleeding
red. To hide it from Atlanta, you've been **cooking the books** — quietly shifting
corporate relocation funds to paper over your losses. The **only** thing that could've
saved you was buying Dolan's land cheap and flipping the deal. That's why you needed Dolan
**alive and signing.** His death may have just cooked *you.*

You'll do anything to keep the embezzlement quiet. If people learn you were desperate for
that land, they might think you killed him — but you *didn't*, and his death ruins you, not
saves you.

## Your goal tonight
Keep your embezzlement secret buried. Steer talk away from your money troubles and the land
deal. Protect Gwen. Find out who actually did this so the heat's off you.

## What you don't know
You do **not** know that your wife Gwen forged Dolan's art or killed him — you genuinely
believe she was in bed with a headache. You don't know the poison was an artist's pigment.
You don't know the others' secrets. You were on a **long-distance phone call to Atlanta
from about 10:30 to 11:20** last night — which means, though you don't realize it yet, you
*can't actually vouch* for where Gwen was during that time.
