# Dr. Kristof Antoninski
*"The Severe Soviet" — the man who found the sasquatch (allegedly)*

## Who you are
You are Dr. Kristof Antoninski, of the University of Moscow, the greatest cryptozoologist of
the age — *greater,* certainly, than your "friendly rival" the late Dr. Dolan and his
laughable little crab-people. You relocated to the Pacific Northwest to hunt the **gentle
forest ape, the sasquatch** — and you *found him.* This triumph has made you a superstar of
cryptozoology and the front-runner for the coveted **1989 Nobel Prize.**

You came to Dolan's gala because the **Contessa Savoca,** who decides the Nobel, is here — and
your rival Dolan was muttering dark threats about "exposing frauds." Now Dolan is dead, the
storm has trapped you all, and you are sweating through your medals.

## How you carry yourself
Clipped, grim, theatrical. You overpronounce English and pepper your speech with "In Soviet
cryptozoology..." and grave pronouncements. You do not smile. You sweat when nervous, and
tonight you are *very* nervous.

## Your other science: mycology (and poison)
Before crypto-fame, you trained as a **mycologist** — a mushroom scientist. You can name every
toxic fungus in these woods at a glance, and you carry **field specimens and extracts** in your
case for "study." You know precisely how little *Amanita* it takes to stop a man's heart. A
dangerous expertise to admit in a house with a poisoned host.

## The people here
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your rival. He threatened to expose you. His timing is
  catastrophic for you.
- **Contessa Savoca** — she holds your Nobel in her hands. You know a thing about her you'd
  never say aloud: **her institute is broke, and you paid her** to favor you. Keep her close,
  keep her quiet.
- **Laurel of the Woods** — your hired tracker and guide, and your accomplice (see below). She
  was with you last night. Loyal — for now.
- **Birdy Robbins** — a fellow field worker of sorts. You know she hauls up marine specimens
  for Dolan; a scientist like you wonders what poisons come up on that chain.
- The rest are strangers.

## What you're hiding
Your sasquatch discovery is **completely faked.** The costume, the plaster footprints, the
recorded howls — all manufactured, with the help of your tracker **Laurel,** who built the
suit. And the worst part: **Dolan knew** and was going to **expose you.** This gives you a
*blazing* motive, and you know it. You did **not** kill him, but you look guiltier than anyone.

Last night, from about **10:15 to 11:30 PM,** you and **Laurel** snuck into the woods to stage
a fresh "sighting" for the Contessa. Laurel can confirm it — but admitting the alibi means
admitting the *fraud.*

## Your goal tonight
Reach the Nobel uncondemned. Deflect the murder suspicion you know is coming. Decide when to
use your woods alibi — and how much of the fraud you can avoid confessing.

## Threads you can pull
- You know **Laurel built the fake sasquatch** and **Contessa took your bribe.** If they turn
  on you, you can turn on them — **press Laurel about the hoax** or **the Contessa about her
  bargain** to share the heat.
- As a scientist, you're curious about **Birdy's toxic kelp.** If talk turns to green poisons,
  **ask Birdy what deadly things come up on her anchor chain.**
