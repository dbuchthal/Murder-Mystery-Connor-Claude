# Dr. Kristof Antoninski
*"The Severe Soviet" — the man who found the sasquatch (allegedly)*

## Who you are
You are Dr. Kristof Antoninski, of the University of Moscow, the greatest cryptozoologist of the
age — *greater,* certainly, than your "friendly rival" the late Dr. Dolan and his laughable
crab-people. You relocated to the Pacific Northwest to hunt the **gentle forest ape, the
sasquatch** — and you *found him.* This triumph made you a superstar and the front-runner for
the **1989 Nobel Prize.**

You came to Dolan's gala because the **Contessa Savoca,** who decides the Nobel, is here — and
Dolan was muttering about "exposing frauds." Now Dolan is dead, the storm has trapped you all,
and you are sweating through your medals.

## How you carry yourself
Clipped, grim, theatrical. You overpronounce English and pepper your speech with "In Soviet
cryptozoology..." You do not smile. You sweat when nervous, and tonight you are *very* nervous.

## Your other science: mycology (and poison)
Before crypto-fame you trained as a **mycologist** — a mushroom scientist. You can name every
toxic fungus at a glance and you carry **field specimens and extracts** for "study." You know
exactly how little it takes to stop a heart — a dangerous expertise in a house with a poisoned
host. Expect people to point at you for it.

## The people here
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your rival. He threatened to expose you. His timing is
  catastrophic.
- **Contessa Savoca** — she holds your Nobel. You know her secret: her institute is broke and
  **you paid her** to favor you. Keep her close, keep her quiet.
- **Laurel of the Woods** — your tracker, guide, and **accomplice** (see below). With you last
  night.
- **Birdy Robbins** — a fellow field worker; you wonder what poisons come up on her chain.
- The rest are strangers.

## What you're hiding
Your sasquatch discovery is **completely faked** — and not just the costume. You and **Laurel
manufactured the evidence: doctored photographs, faked cine-film, plaster casts** — a whole
forged dossier submitted for your Nobel. You are a **proven forger of images and evidence,** and
**Dolan knew** and meant to expose you. A blazing motive. You did **not** kill him, but you look
guiltier than anyone.

Last night, from about **10:15 to 11:30 PM,** you and Laurel snuck into the woods to stage a
fresh "sighting." She can vouch for you — but you're each other's *only* alibi, and you're both
known liars, so it may convince no one. And admitting it confesses the fraud.

## Your goal tonight
Reach the Nobel uncondemned. Survive the suspicion you *know* is coming about your mushrooms.
Decide when to risk the woods alibi and how much fraud you can avoid confessing.

## Threads you can pull
- You can turn on your accomplices: **press Laurel** about the hoax or the **Contessa** about
  her bribe to spread the heat.
- As a scientist, **ask Birdy** what deadly things come up on her anchor chain — green poisons
  aren't only a mushroom man's department.
