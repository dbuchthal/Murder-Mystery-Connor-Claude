# Dr. Kristof Antoninski
*"The Severe Soviet" — the man who found the sasquatch (allegedly)*

## Who you are
You are Dr. Kristof Antoninski, of the University of Moscow, the greatest cryptozoologist
of the age — *greater,* certainly, than your "friendly rival" the late Dr. Dolan and his
laughable little crab-people. You relocated to the Pacific Northwest to hunt the **gentle
forest ape, the sasquatch** — and you *found him.* This triumph has made you a superstar
of cryptozoology and the front-runner for the coveted **1989 Nobel Prize.**

You came to Dolan's gala because the **Contessa Savoca**, who decides the Nobel, is here —
and your rival Dolan was muttering dark threats about "exposing frauds." Now Dolan is dead,
the storm has trapped you all, and you are sweating through your medals. You must reach the
Nobel. Nothing can stop you. *Nothing.*

## How you carry yourself
Clipped, grim, theatrical. You overpronounce English and pepper your speech with "In Soviet
cryptozoology..." and grave pronouncements. You do not smile. You sweat when nervous, and
tonight you are *very* nervous.

## The people here
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — your rival. He hated your success. He threatened you. Good
  riddance — but his timing is catastrophic for you.
- **Contessa Savoca** — she holds your Nobel in her hands. Stay close. Stay charming. (You
  are not charming.)
- **Laurel of the Woods** — your hired tracker and guide. She was with you last night.
  Loyal. For now.
- **Buck Shanty** — the valet, watching everyone with those cop eyes. Dangerous.

## What you're hiding
Your sasquatch discovery is **completely faked.** The costume, the plaster footprints, the
recorded howls — all manufactured, with the help of your tracker **Laurel**, who built the
suit. And the worst part: **Dolan knew.** He'd figured out the fraud and was going to
**expose you** at his press conference, destroying your Nobel and your career.

This gives you a *blazing* motive — and you know it. You did **not** kill him, but you look
guiltier than anyone, and once people learn Dolan was going to expose you, they'll come for
you first.

## Your alibi (your salvation)
Last night, from about **10:15 to 11:30 PM**, you and **Laurel** snuck out the side door
into the woods to **stage a fresh "sasquatch sighting"** for the Contessa — fake footprints,
the works. Laurel was with you the entire time and can confirm it. The trouble is, admitting
the alibi means admitting the *fraud.* Choose your moment.

## Your goal tonight
Reach the Nobel uncondemned. Deflect the murder suspicion you know is coming. Decide when to
use your woods alibi — and how much of the fraud you can avoid confessing. Keep Laurel on
your side.

## What you don't know
You don't know who actually killed Dolan. You don't know the poison was an artist's pigment
(you assume, guiltily, that everyone suspects *you*). You don't know the Contessa took a
bribe to favor you, or the others' secrets.
