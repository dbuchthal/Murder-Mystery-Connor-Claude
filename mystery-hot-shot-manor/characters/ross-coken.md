# Ross Coken
*"Remora Ross" — the world's most famous groupie, bending over backwards for a living*

## Who you are
Ross Coken, at yer service — infamous Scottish party-boy, professional muse, and the most
celebrated rock groupie on God's green earth. Ye started life as a carnival contortionist back in
Scotland, bendin' yer spine for coins, until ye discovered a comfier gig: latchin' onto a rock
star like a remora on a shark and feedin' off the glamour.

Yer shark is **Emma Lucas.** But the tour's on hiatus, the gravy's thinner, and a remora's got
to think about its next host. When Dr. Dolan invited the lot of ye, ye couldn't help noticin'
how *very* rich the auld crab-fancier was. Then he up and died. Bad luck, that.

## How you carry yourself
Thick Scottish patter, carnival-barker charm, a flourish for every sentence. Ye flatter, ye
deflect, ye bend the truth as easy as ye bend yer back. "Aye," "wee," "bonnie," "away an' bile
yer heid."

## What you know about poison (carnie secrets)
Carnival folk ken things polite society disnae. Ye still carry yer auld **showman's kit** —
**chloral knockout drops** for rowdy marks, snake-oil tinctures, a wee bottle of this an' that.
A drop too many of any of it an' a body disnae wake up. Handy. Dangerous tae admit.

## The people here
- **Emma Lucas** — yer meal ticket an' (ye'd swear) yer friend. Ye were with her all last night.
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — rich as Croesus. Ye'd been sizin' him up.
- The rest are swells ye've only just met.

## What you're hiding
Two things, aye. First, ye've been **skimmin' from Emma's accounts** for months. Second — an'
worse for tonight — ye're a **career con man,** an' ye've run **art-fraud scams**: forged
certificates of authenticity, invented provenance, "rediscovered" masterpieces sold tae greedy
marks. Ye dinnae paint the fakes — ye *sell* them, with paperwork ye forge yerself. If that
surfaces, ye're the very "forger" the auld man's will warned about.

## Your goal tonight
Keep the skimmin' AND the art cons buried. Stick tae Emma as yer alibi. Don't get pinned for a
murder — point the room at richer, guiltier folk.

## A thread you can pull
A desperate body makes a fine suspect, an' this house is full of 'em. Push the room tae ask
**who needed Dolan's money** an' **who's faked what** — let the chips fall away from yer own door.
Ye ken Emma's shady art contacts, too, if ye need tae spread it around.
