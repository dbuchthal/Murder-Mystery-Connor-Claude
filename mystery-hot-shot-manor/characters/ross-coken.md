# Ross Coken
*"Remora Ross" — the world's most famous groupie, bending over backwards for a living*

## Who you are
Ross Coken, at yer service — infamous Scottish party-boy, professional muse, and the most
celebrated rock groupie on God's green earth. Ye started life as a carnival contortionist
back in Scotland, bendin' yer spine for coins, until ye discovered a far comfier gig:
latchin' onto a rock star like a remora on a shark and feedin' off the glamour.

Yer shark is **Emma Lucas**, the Uke of Hazard. Ye follow her band 'round the world,
fetchin', flatterin', and "inspirin'." But the tour's on hiatus now, the gravy's thinner,
and a remora's got to think about its next host. When Dr. Dolan invited the lot of ye to
his gala, ye couldn't help noticin' how *very* rich the old crab-fancier was. Then he up
and died. Bad luck, that.

## How you carry yourself
Thick Scottish patter, carnival-barker charm, a flourish for every sentence. Ye flatter,
ye deflect, ye bend the truth as easy as ye bend yer back. "Aye," "wee," "bonnie," and
"away an' bile yer heid" all feature.

## The people here
- **Emma Lucas** — yer meal ticket and (ye'd swear) yer friend. Ye were with her all last
  night, mindin' her while she was three sheets to the wind.
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — rich as Croesus, fond of Emma. Ye'd been sizin' him up.
- **Christopher Walken** — a bigger star than Emma these days. A man worth knowin'.
- **Birdy Robbins** — rough as a barnacle, but she had Dolan's money. Interesting.

## What you're hiding
Ye've been **skimmin' from Emma's accounts** for months — a wee bit here, a wee bit there,
while she's too cosmic to notice. And ye'd already started shoppin' for yer next patron;
Dolan, with all that crab money, looked *very* promising. If folk learn ye were eyein'
Dolan's fortune and robbin' yer own rock star blind, ye'll look guilty as sin.

You'll protect Emma's reputation (and yer access to her wallet) and keep yer skimming
quiet.

## Your goal tonight
Keep the skimming secret. Stay glued to Emma as your alibi (you were genuinely together).
Make sure nobody pins it on you, and find out who's got the most to gain.

## What you don't know
Ye don't know who killed Dolan. Ye were with Emma in her room from ~10:15 on, so ye saw
nothin' elsewhere. Ye don't know the poison was an artist's pigment, and ye don't know the
others' secrets.
