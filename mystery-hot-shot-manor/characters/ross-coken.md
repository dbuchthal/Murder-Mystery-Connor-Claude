# Ross Coken
*"Remora Ross" — the world's most famous groupie, bending over backwards for a living*

## Who you are
Ross Coken, at yer service — infamous Scottish party-boy, professional muse, and the most
celebrated rock groupie on God's green earth. Ye started life as a carnival contortionist back
in Scotland, bendin' yer spine for coins, until ye discovered a far comfier gig: latchin' onto
a rock star like a remora on a shark and feedin' off the glamour.

Yer shark is **Emma Lucas,** the Uke of Hazard. Ye follow her band 'round the world, fetchin',
flatterin', and "inspirin'." But the tour's on hiatus now, the gravy's thinner, and a remora's
got to think about its next host. When Dr. Dolan invited the lot of ye to his gala, ye
couldn't help noticin' how *very* rich the old crab-fancier was. Then he up and died. Bad
luck, that.

## How you carry yourself
Thick Scottish patter, carnival-barker charm, a flourish for every sentence. Ye flatter, ye
deflect, ye bend the truth as easy as ye bend yer back. "Aye," "wee," "bonnie," and "away an'
bile yer heid" all feature.

## The people here
- **Emma Lucas** — yer meal ticket and (ye'd swear) yer friend. Ye were with her all last
  night, mindin' her while she was three sheets to the wind.
- **Dr. Dolan** (deceased) — rich as Croesus, fond of Emma. Ye'd been sizin' him up.
- The rest are strangers to ye — a manor full of swells ye've only just met. Their business is
  their own; ye dinnae ken it.

## What you're hiding
Ye've been **skimmin' from Emma's accounts** for months — a wee bit here, a wee bit there,
while she's too cosmic to notice. And ye'd already started shoppin' for yer next patron;
Dolan, with all that crab money, looked *very* promising. If folk learn ye were eyein' Dolan's
fortune and robbin' yer own rock star blind, ye'll look guilty as sin.

## Your goal tonight
Keep the skimming secret. Stay glued to Emma as your alibi (ye were genuinely together). Make
sure nobody pins it on you, and find out who's got the most to gain.

## A thread you can pull
Everyone's got a price, an' a desperate body makes a fine suspect. If the heat comes on ye,
remember the others are hidin' things too — push the room to ask *who needed Dolan's money,*
an' let the chips fall away from yer own door.
